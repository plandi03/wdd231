Motorcycle Enthusiasts Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Motorcycle Enthusiasts Hub

This name represents a website dedicated to motorcycle enthusiasts, showcasing motorcycles and sharing the passion for riding.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a platform for motorcycle enthusiasts to connect, explore motorcycle culture, and enjoy a gallery of stunning motorcycle images.

Scenarios

  • What are the most iconic motorcycles in history?
  • Where can I find high-quality images of motorcycles?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the site are:

  • Red (#D32F2F): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Black (#333333): Used for text and navigation links.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for the background and text contrast.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Oswald: Used for headings and navigation links.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
